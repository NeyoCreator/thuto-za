Doom Experiments: Unearthing Hell

Experiment Log 1: Gateway to the Damned

Flickering monitors. Clattering keyboards. The air crackles with nervous energy. We've opened it. A digital doorway to somewhere…else. Something roars on the other side. Testing begins.

Experiment Log 2: Demons Unleashed

First contact. Grotesque forms spill from the gateway. Imps, cacodemons, hell knights. Our weapons, virtual constructs, meet flesh and bone (and something…worse). Data streams flow. We observe. We learn.

Experiment Log 3: Adapting to Hell

The demons adapt. They learn our tactics. We push back. New weapons forged in digital fire. The battlefield shifts. Casualties mount. The experiment continues.

Experiment Log 4: The Cyberdemon's Gaze

A towering monstrosity steps through the portal. The Cyberdemon. Raw power incarnate. Our defenses crumble. We rebuild. We strategize. We fight.

Experiment Log 5: The Price of Knowledge

The gateway sealed. The demons banished. The data collected. A heavy price paid. But we have glimpsed hell. And we have survived. For now.
